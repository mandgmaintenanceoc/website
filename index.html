Commercial, Janitorial, Office, Medical & Floor Care

M&G Maintenance is a family-owned business providing thorough, reliable cleaning and maintenance for businesses and institutions across the region. We focus on commercial and institutional clients—offices, medical facilities, retail, and more—and we also consider select residential inquiries. Contact us to discuss your needs and we’ll put together a plan that fits your space and schedule. We offer flexible scheduling (nightly, daily, or weekly) and are available around the clock for urgent requests.

Commercial Cleaning

Our team creates customized commercial cleaning plans for your facility. Whether you need nightly, daily, or weekly service, we tailor our approach to keep your building clean and presentable for employees and visitors.

Janitorial Services

We handle everyday janitorial duties so your workspaces, break rooms, restrooms, kitchens, and floors stay clean and sanitary. Consistent janitorial care keeps your space looking professional for colleagues, clients, and customers.

Office Cleaning

Every office has different needs. We work with you to design an office cleaning plan that keeps your space immaculate, helps reduce the spread of germs, and supports a productive work environment.

Medical & Healthcare Facilities

Patient and visitor safety is paramount. We provide cleaning and disinfecting services suited to healthcare settings, with attention to the specific protocols and guidelines that keep medical facilities safe and compliant.

Floor Stripping & Waxing

Well-maintained floors make a strong impression. Our staff strip, refinish, and wax hard-surface floors to protect them and extend their life, so your facility always looks its best.

Carpet Cleaning

Carpets in lobbies, hallways, and conference rooms collect dirt and allergens. We deep-clean carpets to remove embedded soil and stains, restore appearance, and help extend the life of your carpet.

Nightly, Daily & Weekly Programs

We assess your property and recommend a schedule that works for you—nightly, daily, or weekly. You get a cleaning plan that matches your operations and keeps your location consistently cared for.

Touch Point Cleaning & Disinfectant Misting

We clean high-touch surfaces—door handles, elevator buttons, keyboards, and more—and offer disinfectant misting to reduce the spread of germs, bacteria, and pathogens in your space.
