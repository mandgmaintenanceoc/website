Why Work With Us

We are a family-owned business built on trust, respect, and hard work. When you join M&G Maintenance, you become part of a team that takes pride in the work we do and the clients we serve throughout Orange County and surrounding areas.

Steady Work

Our clients are businesses, offices, and medical facilities that rely on us consistently. That means reliable, recurring hours for our team members.

Supportive Team Environment

We provide on-the-job training and work alongside our employees to make sure everyone has what they need to succeed. No prior experience required—just a strong work ethic and a good attitude.

Room to Grow

We promote from within whenever possible. Many of our team leads started in entry-level cleaning positions. If you're dependable and motivated, there's a path forward here.
