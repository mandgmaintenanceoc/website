About M&G Maintenance LLC

A family-owned commercial cleaning and property maintenance company serving Orange County.

Who We Are

M&G Maintenance LLC specializes in commercial janitorial and comprehensive property maintenance solutions for businesses, office spaces, and property management needs. Our experienced team ensures your property always makes a professional impression—clean, safe, and ready for employees, tenants, or guests.

Our foundation is built on transparent communication, clear scopes of work, and rigorous quality-control procedures. Leveraging operational tools, robust checklists, and proven systems, we maintain consistency and accountability across every service, from turnover cleaning to ongoing maintenance. With M&G, you can trust your property is in expert hands.

Our Mission

Our mission at M&G Maintenance LLC is to provide property owners, managers, and businesses with seamless, reliable, and structured cleaning and maintenance solutions.
